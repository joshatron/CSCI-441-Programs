Controls

Shape Controls:
    q- square
    w- circle
    e- triangle
    r- lines

Fill Controls:
    a- uniform fill
    s- random fill

Resizing Controls:
    z- fit width
    x- fit height
    c- fit both
    v- same size

Misc Controls:
    u- toggle background
    i- toggle direct color vs random near color
    o- clear screen
    p- toggle follow mouse
    scroll- make larger or smaller
