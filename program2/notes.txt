shape- holds info about shape
    brick locations and angles relative to shape center
    center location
    internally
        x diameter
        y diameter
        height
        # of sides
        angle to rotate everything

    to update values, pass it brick width, brick height, brick depth, spacing, scale, number of bricks deep

structure- holds vector of shapes and transforms them to be correctly created in relation to each other
    vector of mat4 for each brick
    mat4 for individual brick shape
    mat4 for light source
    location, color, and brightness for light source

    to update values, pass it brick width, brick height, brick depth, spacing, scale, number of bricks deep
